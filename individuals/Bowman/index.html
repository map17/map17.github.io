My subject matter is nature, my work seeks to take familiar animals and make them unfamiliar. I predominantly use animal exoskeletons to create intriguing delicate images, where the viewer's perception of nature is challenged. The works are preoccupied with the notion of the in-between; the repurposing of animals in taxidermy or material items has a stage before the final product, where the animal is hung in the balance of its former life and its metamorphosis into a new life.
