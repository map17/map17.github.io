"One day I woke up, still in my dream, confused, and I did not know where I was - London or Tokyo. There was a feeling of being somewhere but I could not remember if it was from my childhood or a recent memory."

In Meet You There Tomoko Suwa-Krull explores mental landscapes of people she meets and how they relate to the environment they live in. By photographing scenery and persons separately, she portrays her subjects' mental landscapes. The combination of large-scale wallpaper and framed profiles shares this experience with the viewer, whose stories are observing our emotions, transforming us, leaving impressions and encouraging us to create our own stories.
