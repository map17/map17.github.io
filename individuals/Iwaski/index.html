You have seen this before. In another place, another time. I have been there with you, maybe as a different person, with different hair and different eyes.

I have cut up and reassembled it to give it a new home. But that home was already there. It was already there and still isn't at the same time. Is that where it belongs?

I am here. Are you? Hello, goodbye. It is my body, and not mine.
