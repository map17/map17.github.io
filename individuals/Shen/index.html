How to distinguish between the dream and reality? The artist had researched on different religions, they all mention about who know the LOGOS, then know the truth. When human observe their inner world, they will observe imperceptibility and energy. When people more concentrated and focus, they will close to the "truth". Meanwhile, the artist inspired by synaesthesia which refers to a synthesis or intermingling of the senses. Synesthetes can see sounds as colours or shapes or associate qualities of one sense with another, it hides deeply in human mind and it is a formulation or statement of human inner world. Therefore, colour photogram is similar with synaesthesia, objects touch the sensitive paper in the darkroom, the light will be reflexed to different colour lights and recorded by sensitive paper, thus, that is why using it and this abstract way to represent a complex philosophical idea and to explore the limited view of the reality.

People live in a world that full of image and information. There are overwhelmed things coming to our mind and life day after day, disrupt and influence our connection to ourselves. The artist wants to break the boundary between the reality and 2D image, use this work to discuss the relationship between outside world and human inner world: we absented the experience of our own, return to ourselves.
