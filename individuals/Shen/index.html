Ziheng Shen uses photography, moving image and installation to investigate the relationship between humans' outer and inner worlds. This work was made by the photogram of the Fluid Dynamics Art. In oriental culture, Fluidism art is based on fluid dynamics patterns which largely determine all life as a result, it presents nature's most fundamental forms of beauty.

He spilled the pigment on the transparent films randomly and observed the liquid creating the shapes itself. In this process, he was in the state of Mindfulness and related to subconsciousness and inner world, which means he was fully present and aware. He observed his thoughts and feelings without judging them good or bad, and mediate the audience to the state of Mindfulness through artwork, help them living in the moment and awakening to the current experience, rather than dwelling on the past or anticipating the future.

The whole work doesn't have objective images from this world and combine with photogram, to encourage audience to stimulate their subjective world and remind them to think about the authenticity of this world.
