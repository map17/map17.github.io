Yudong Zhang works with portrait photography and still life images, exploring the boundaries of photography, art and the archive. In Surroundings (2017), he focuses on the Chinese students who surround him, probing into their lives and using different perspectives to objectively record every detail of their disordered rooms, from an intimate angle, capturing unconscious facial expressions and gestures. Still, each of his photographs is connected with the subjective flow of his memories. Collecting the past is collecting facts. He believes that an individual's story is just a story, but a thousand stories constitute a history.
