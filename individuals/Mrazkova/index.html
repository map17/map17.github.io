At Arm's Length is a personal visual story where Barbora Mrazkova explores themes of intimacy and connection, through collaboration and confrontation in front of and behind the camera.

By taking pictures of both strangers and friends Barbora depicts different forms of intimacy built upon different levels of intensity shared with her subjects. Employing a documentary approach, still-life and portraiture are combined to question the authenticity of intimacy.

At heart, this body of work is a photographer's attempt to get closer to her subjects yet to remain at a distance; to analyse the nature of her subjects and how connections may be drawn between them, both visually and emotionally.
