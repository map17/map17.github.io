Project Title

Julie Derbyshire

❮ ❯

Julie's practice is concerned with fragility and transience and reflects upon both the vicissitudes of the human condition and humankind's impact on the earth.

With these underlying concerns in mind, Julie works within the environment of renewed engagement with objects and materiality and has an ongoing interest in the photograph as object. Exploring both two and three dimensionality, she considers questions around the photographic and the nature of representation, ways in which the photograph may function, what a photograph can be and can do. Materiality is investigated and process and "making" are embedded in her work playing an important role in the resolved outcomes.
