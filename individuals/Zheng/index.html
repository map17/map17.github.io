Xi Zheng is a photographer and visual artist who researches the concept of the face as a medium. She explores the creative possibilities of the face in which remediation and mediation are vital processes.

Her project Beyond the Face (2017), blends EEG brain waves with close-up photographs of chins to create a facial landscape alluding to Eastern aesthetics. She moves beyond the boundaries of the ghost and the shell to challenge the idea that the outer is a reflection of the inner person.
