Agnès Villette is a French journalist and photographer, based in London. She has been a contributor to Citizen K magazine for the past 23 years, she is also freelancing for various French and British magazines, such as Le Monde, Wedemain, Dust, Le Temps, Le Vif. Her published work and research are rooted in issues raised by the anthropocene. She is investigating how the environment and social groups are impacted by toxic waste, globalisation, nuclear energy. She has published and exhibited several photo projects and is currently studying for a Master in Photography at LCC and developing projects on Dark Ecology. Her MA project is investigating our nuclear age and consciousness, through notions of deep time, memory, data monitoring and the unconscious.
