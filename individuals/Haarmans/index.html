This body of work explores the notion of fetishism and how an object can fulfil a certain human pleasure. This project aims to bring together the male and female desire in a gender non-conforming area. These vacuum formed sculptures contain everyday objects that trigger a sexual desire within the viewer. They play with our fantasies and unveil our fetishes, recalling abstract moments of pleasure and intimacy. I want the viewer to create a deeper investigation in the abstraction of form, texture and composition, which might lead us to the core of our desires.
