About

LCC's MA Photography programme embraces the diversity of visual expression, across media and genres, that is contemporary photographic practice. As the class of 2017, we come from an international range of cultural and experiential backgrounds, and over the past year we have been inspiring each other to push the boundaries of modern photography through experimental practice, theoretical contextualisation and active research. An open-minded, progressive and multimedia approach has given us the freedom and autonomy to make thoughtful, bold, provocative and conceptually absorbing works that engage with our current challenging times.
